Basketball Training Habits

How to train smarter during the week

I picked this topic because basketball is a big part of my life, and training habits can change your level a lot. Many players train hard, but they don’t always train in a smart way.

In my opinion, good training is about consistency, recovery, and doing the basics every day. If you only train when you feel motivated, you will be up and down. I try to follow simple routines so I can improve even during busy weeks.

Habits that help me stay consistent

  • Warm up before every workout (mobility + light movement)
  • Train ball-handling for a few minutes daily
  • Take sleep seriously (it’s part of training)
  • Drink water and eat enough protein

My simple workout order

  1. Dynamic warm-up and stretching
  2. Form shooting close to the rim
  3. Game-speed shots (spot-up + off the dribble)
  4. Conditioning or sprints at the end
