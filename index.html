Basketball Training Habits

Simple habits that help you improve

Basketball training is not only about working hard, it is also about training smart. Good habits help you improve faster and stay consistent during the week.

In my opinion, the basics matter the most: warm-up, skill work, and recovery. If you do small work every day, it adds up a lot over time.
