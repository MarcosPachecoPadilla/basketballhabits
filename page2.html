Recovery and staying healthy

Recovery is very important because if you never rest, your body will feel heavy and you can get injured easier. Stretching after training and sleeping enough helps you stay consistent.

I also think it helps to track your workouts. Even small notes like how you felt or what you practiced can help you improve over time.
