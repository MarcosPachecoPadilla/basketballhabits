Recovery, skills, and staying healthy

Something people forget is recovery. If you never rest, your body will feel heavy and you can get injured easier. I learned that training is not only working out, it is also taking care of your body after.

I also think it helps to track your workouts. Even writing down small notes like “made 200 shots” or “felt tired today” can show patterns over time. When I keep it simple, it is easier to stay consistent.

Things I try to do for recovery

I try to stretch after practice, sleep enough, and not ignore pain. If something hurts in a bad way, it’s smarter to rest than to make it worse.
